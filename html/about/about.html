About

This website is used to create an anonymous connection between immigrants and immigration lawyers. The purpose is to assure that all people are given the chance at fair representation at imigration court. Through this website, immigrants can load their personal information so that lawyers can look at their situation and see if these immigrants have a valid case to push for nationilization. There is also the opportunity for family members to notify lawyers that an immigrant is in court and is in need of representation. Finally there is an option for an immigrant in a detention cell to call and an automated system will flag the account and record the phone number to localize the immigrant so that lawyers have a list of people that need representation, and where they are located. The database created is encrypted and anonymous.
